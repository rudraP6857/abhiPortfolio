Ethical Analysis: This situation presented serious ethical concerns about fairness, access to quality healthcare, and the potential for AI systems to perpetuate existing healthcare disparities. I realized that technical accuracy alone is insufficient‚Äîwe must ensure equitable performance across all population segments.

Personal Reflection: This experience fundamentally shaped my understanding of technology's responsibility. As developers, we don't just write code‚Äîwe shape how society functions. I learned that ethical considerations must be integrated from the beginning of any project, not added as an afterthought.

My Commitment: I pledge to always prioritize ethical considerations in my work, advocate for inclusive design practices, and speak up when I identify potential harm in technological solutions.
